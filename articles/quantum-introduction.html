Introduction

🎙 A Quantum (and a bit messy) Introduction

I'm not really good at this part of things, so let's keep it simple and quick.

2026 · SaraQ

My name is SaraQ, I'm 36 years old (♫♫♫ Hiii Saraaaa ♫♫♫), and I recently discovered, on my own (with a lot of assisted exploration), that I'm neuroatypical.

No, I'm not saying it for pity (please don't lol), but rather as a heads-up:

💡 My brain is wired differently.
In a way I find very positive, but also very... exhausting ahahah
🧠 I have a brain that runs in a constant loop
Often at 3000 km/h (or just 1000 when I'm life-burned out). And the best way to explain it is through quantum physics: I have what I call mental superpositions. Every thought is there, electric, floating in the background, ready to activate if I choose to focus on it. They freeze like particles… but only when I give them my attention.
🎯 The result?
I often throw out punchlines, angry rants, or cosmic theories nobody asked for, but which perhaps deserve to exist.

Why this blog?

Because I thought to myself:

  • What if this stylish chaos could speak to someone else?
  • What if my reflections, my humor, or my mental tornadoes found an echo somewhere?

So here you are, it's a pleasure to welcome you here.
Welcome to my space. Have fun, and rock on 🤘🏽
